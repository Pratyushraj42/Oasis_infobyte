OVERVIEW

Panipat (Haryana) is a renowned Textile manufacturing hub, famous for the production of curtains, blankets, bedding, carpets and other home furnishing products catering to domestic and overseas markets THE VILLAGE BUSINESS PARK is an industrial plotting colony which is duly approved by DTCP Haryana. The Township is spread over an area of more than 100 acres. The township offers a wide range of plots ranging from 466 Sq. Meters (576.43 Sq. Yards) to 4399 Sq Meters (5189.84 Sq. Yards). Located on Chautala Road, opposite sector 29, part 2, HUDA, Panipat (Haryana).

OUR VISION

India has a vast potential to be a global manufacturer with its huge young workforce. Our vision is to create a world class infrastructure to sill and enhance the talent of our youths and entrepreneurs to transform India into a modern yet competitive manufacturing hub.
